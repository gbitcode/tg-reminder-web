Don4Get Reminder Bot

Features

💬

Any Message

Forward messages, send text, images, files, voice notes - anything becomes a reminder.

⏰

Quick Presets

One-tap buttons for common times: 30 min, 1 hour, 2pm, tomorrow, next week.

🗣

Natural Language

Type "tomorrow at 3pm" or "in 2 hours" - the bot understands you.

🌐

Your Timezone

Set your timezone once and all reminders work in your local time.

🔔

Snooze & Postpone

Not ready? Postpone any reminder with a single tap.

📝

Manage Easily

View, edit, or delete your reminders anytime with /reminders command.

How It Works

1

Send or Forward

Send any message to the bot or forward a message you want to be reminded about.

2

Pick a Time

Choose from preset buttons or type a custom time like "friday 5pm".

3

Get Reminded

The bot sends you the message at the scheduled time with options to mark done or postpone.
